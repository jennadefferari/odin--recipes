Easy Vegan Pancakes

Description

Pancakes are a saturday morning staple, and this recipe delivers light and fluffy vegan pancakes that will rock your breakfast routine. The key word here is fast, as these puppies will be done quicker than your boyfriend on prom night, hopefully with slightly less guilt though. enjoy!

Ingredients

  • 1 cup all purpose flour
  • 2 tablesspoons stevia
  • 2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 cup of unsweetened applesauce
  • 1 cup soymilk
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla

Steps

  1. in a large bowl, mix flour, stevia, baking powder, and salt. mix thoroughly before making a well in the center. Then add your milk, applesauce, vanilla, and oil. Mix until smooth; definatley do not want lumps.
  2. Heat a griddle or frying pan on medium heat. light oil or spray the pan. After a moment, check if your pan is ready by dropping a tiny amount of batter on the pan and checking to see if it cooks. Once the pan is ready, spoon batter on using a spoon or scoop. you can control the size of the pancakes here. personally, I enjoy mini pancakes and use about a spoonful. However, if you want standard sized pancakes use approximatley 1/4 cup for each pancake.
  3. Cook each pancake on either side, browning both. if you are unsure when your pancake is ready to flip, look for bubbles forming in the batter. flip when at least a few bubbles have formed and give it about a minute on it's other side.
  4. Serve these bad boys hot with vegan butter and sugarfree syrup.
